The Matrix Revolutions

In a dystopia overrun by robots, Neo, mankind's greatest hope, is trapped in a limbo world.

Meanwhile, the majority of the planet's population remains in a state of suspended virtual reality. The few humans who are cognizant of the grim realities of the world desperately try to hold off their mechanical enemies long enough for Neo to escape and save the day, but things turn disastrous when all-powerful computer program Agent Smith arrives in the flesh.
